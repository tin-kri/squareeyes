About SquareEyes

Our Story

What started as a shared passion among four founders quickly became a vision for a better streaming experience. Frustrated by endless scrolling, fragmented libraries, and impersonal recommendations, we set out to create a platform where discovering great entertainment feels effortless. Combining expertise in technology, design, and digital media, we built a service focused on simplicity, personalization, and quality. Our goal has always been to connect audiences with stories that entertain, inspire, and bring people together, while creating a streaming experience that feels modern, intuitive, and enjoyable from the very first click.

The Mission

Our mission is to make discovering great entertainment simple, personalized, and accessible. We strive to connect viewers with stories they'll love through innovative technology, thoughtful design, and a carefully curated content experience. By putting audiences first, we aim to create meaningful moments of entertainment for people everywhere.

What we Offer

We deliver a modern streaming experience designed to help viewers discover, enjoy, and connect with great entertainment. Our library spans genres, countries, and styles so there's always something new to discover.

  • A modern streaming experience
  • A library of movies and series
  • Seamless streaming across devices, anytime and anywhere
