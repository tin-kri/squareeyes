About SquareEyes

It all started at film school where a group of passionate movie enthusiasts met and discovered out shared love of independent films. As we explored the world of cinema, we quickly realized that there was a need for a platform that could showcase the work of independent filmmakers and connect them with their audiences.

We are committed to showcasing films that are unique and thought-provoking.

Our platform is dedicated to a diverse range of independent movies, which allows us to cater to a variety of tastes and preferences. Our goal is to offer viewers a refreshing alternative to the mainstream movies that dominate most streaming services.
