Colorful Murakami

Kumiko Okada, Japan 2020

Discover the life and work of legendary author Haruki Murakami in this captivating documentary. Gain insights into his unique writing style and the themes that permeate his novels, including alienation, loneliness, and the search for meaning in a chaotic world. With stunning visuals and a thought-provoking narrative, this film is a must-see for anyone interested in literature and the creative arts.
